I'll try to cover the most relevant news, releases, games, tools, and anything else relevant. Please, if you have any recommendations or, any idea you can send me an email to jose@albizures.com or, even better, you can open a PR in [GitHub](https://github.com/albizures/ThisWeekInGodot).
