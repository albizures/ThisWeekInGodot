Hello everyone, welcome to the #26 issue of _This Week in Godot_, Godot is a popular Free and Open Source game development engine and toolset. And this is a weekly overview of what happened the past week in the Godot engine and its community right in your email. I'll try to cover the most relevant news, releases, games, tools, and anything else relevant. Please, if you have any recommendations or, any idea you can send me an email to albizures@vk.com or, even better, you can open a PR in [GitHub](https://github.com/albizures/ThisWeekInGodot).
